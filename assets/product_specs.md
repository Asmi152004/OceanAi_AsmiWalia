# Product Specifications: E-Shop Checkout

## Discount Codes
- **SAVE15**: Applies a 15% discount to the total cart value (excluding shipping).
- **WELCOME10**: Applies a $10 flat discount. (Note: Not implemented in HTML demo, but good for test cases).

## Shipping
- **Standard Shipping**: Free. Delivery in 5-7 business days.
- **Express Shipping**: Costs $10. Delivery in 1-2 business days.

## Cart Limits
- Maximum quantity per item is 5.
- Minimum cart value for checkout is $10.

## Payment
- Supported methods: Credit Card, PayPal.
- Credit Card requires valid number (Luhn algorithm check - mock requirement).
